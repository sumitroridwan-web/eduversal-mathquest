# A Cake to Share — painted art

These `.jpg` files are **auto-generated placeholders** (rasterised from the
book's SVG). Replace them with real painted illustrations to upgrade the book.

| File | What it is | Ratio |
|------|------------|-------|
| `cover.jpg` | front cover | portrait 3:4 |
| `p1.jpg` … `p5.jpg` | story pages 1–5 | landscape ~3:2 |

Keep the **same file names**. No text baked into the images — the app overlays
the sentences. Generation prompts and the character bible are in
`docs/illustration-guide.md`. If you switch to WebP, update `imageExt` on the
book in `src/data/storybookSample.tsx`.
